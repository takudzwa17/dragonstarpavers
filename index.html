Building The Future On Solid Foundation

We pride ourselves in being one of the most trusted brands for quality, durable and high-strength pavers and bricks in Zimbabwe.

Why choose us

Dragon Star Pavers and Bricks is a leading supplier of high quality paving and brick products in Harare, Zimbabwe. Established on May 10, 2023, our company is committed to providing top-notch products at competitive prices.

With a wide range of offerings, including driveway and brick supplies, cement bricks, and various types of pavers, we cater to both residential and commercial customers. Led by a dedicated team of professionals, Dragon Star Pavers and Bricks is your trusted partner for all your paving and brick needs.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of paving and brick solutions. Recognized by our commitment to quality, innovation and customer satisfaction.
